# - Behavioral change is achieved more effectively by soliciting future-oriented suggestions rather than dissecting past performance.

## The idea
An individual selects one behavior to change, describes this objective to a peer, and asks for two specific suggestions for the future.
## Why it matters to me
- ​The receiver must listen attentively and take notes, but is strictly forbidden from critiquing, judging, or rating the advice. The only permitted verbal response to the suggestions is "Thank You".
