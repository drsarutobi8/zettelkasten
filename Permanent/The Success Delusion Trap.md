# The idea
When a behavior produces success, we unconsciously conclude that the behavior caused the success. This creates a trap: we double down on the same habits even when they stop working or start hurting others. The very confidence that drove success becomes the barrier to the next level.

## Why it matters to me
As a founder, I attribute BizVelocity's survival to my 
technical judgment and directness. But those same traits may be limiting how my team operates without me.
